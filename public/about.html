About

Reversi is a two person strategy game. Where players alternate turns and try to place tokens on the board to form a line opposite their own token, but only if an enemy’s token is between them. The intersected opponent is then “captured” and flipped over to one’s own color. The game is over when someone can’t make a legal move or the board is full. Whoever has the most of their color on the board when the game is over wins!
